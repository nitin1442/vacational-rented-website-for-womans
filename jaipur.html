Explore Jaipur

Attractions

Jaipur, known as the Pink City, is famous for its historic forts, palaces, and vibrant culture. Explore iconic landmarks such as the Hawa Mahal, City Palace, and Amer Fort. Visit historical sites like Jantar Mantar and Jal Mahal. Experience the rich heritage of Jaipur by exploring its colorful bazaars and bustling streets.

Accommodations

Discover a range of accommodations in Jaipur, from heritage hotels in historic palaces to budget-friendly guesthouses in the city center. Stay in luxurious hotels with royal amenities or opt for boutique hotels with traditional Rajasthani decor.

Activities

Experience the vibrant culture of Jaipur through various activities. Explore the city's rich history and architecture on guided tours. Enjoy camel rides and elephant safaris in the desert. Shop for traditional handicrafts, textiles, and jewelry in Jaipur's markets.
