Explore Mumbai

Attractions

Mumbai, the financial capital of India, is known for its bustling streets and vibrant culture. Explore iconic landmarks such as the Gateway of India, Marine Drive, and Elephanta Caves. Visit historical sites like Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Vastu Sangrahalaya and Haji Ali Dargah. Experience the hustle and bustle of Mumbai's local markets and bazaars.

Accommodations

Discover a wide range of accommodations in Mumbai, from luxury hotels overlooking the Arabian Sea to budget-friendly hostels in bustling neighborhoods. Stay in upscale hotels in South Mumbai or explore boutique hotels in trendy areas like Bandra and Colaba.

Activities

Experience the vibrant culture of Mumbai through various activities. Explore the city's thriving arts scene by visiting galleries and theaters. Enjoy delicious street food at iconic spots like Juhu Beach and Chowpatty. Take a cruise along the Arabian Sea or explore the city's nightlife in trendy bars and clubs.
